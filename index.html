Notre Histoire

Dairy for everyday life,
crafted in France.

Maison Malai was born from a simple dream: to bring the reliable, nourishing dairy products that millions love in India to every kitchen in France. Founded by Abhimanyu, our mission is to make wholesome dairy as accessible as a morning baguette.

Inspired by Amul's legacy of trust and quality, we are building Maison Malai as France's everyday dairy companion — honest ingredients, fair prices, and the warmth of a home kitchen in every pack.

A
Abhimanyu
CEO & Founder, Maison Malai
